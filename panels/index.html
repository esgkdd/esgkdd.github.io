ESG day panelists

Jingyuan Zhao
AI & Analytics APAC
Capgemini
Dr. Jingyuan is leading Capgemini AI & Analytics APAC, building end-to-end AI solutions and delivering business outcomes at scale with data and AI. She is an experienced analytics and AI professional and has held leadership roles across different industries over the past 15 years, which includes A*STAR research institutes, Nielsen, SingTel, Lazada/Alibaba group and NTUC Enterprise. She brings a unique mix of experiences in data science, big data, data monetization and business strategy. Before joining Capgemini, she was SVP of NTUC group, heading the group's advanced analytics center to build extensive data science capabilities for the group, and shape and transform their business using data-driven strategies. In addition, she was VP of regional data science for Lazada, Alibaba group. At Lazada, she built and led a data science team, working on leading techniques and business solutions to deliver innovative and scalable data products for internal business lines and external partners. Her subject matter expertise includes a deep knowledge of the Consumer Products and Retail sector with a strong focus on customer experience and loyalty programs. She holds a Ph.D. in Statistics from NUS.
Ray Ong Swee Lieng
Regional Sale Director
UBTECH ROBOTICS CORP
Mr. Ray Ong Swee Lieng, Regional Sale Director (Overseas) of UBTECH ROBOTICS CORP and Founder of Youth Champions Academy Singapore, is primarily focusing on proliferating UBTECH End-to-End AI Robotic Education Solution in Asia Pacific, Central Asia and Africa. During his 2 years tenure in UBTECH ROBOTICS CORP, he has developed an extensive network of distributors and educational partners across the regions under his charge. Aligned with the company’s vision, Mr. Ray aims to leverage AI Robotic Education as the key enabler to assist countries in embarking on their Industrial 4.0 movement.

Mr. Ray also led a team of educational expertise in UBTECH to develop a high end and holistic AI Robotic Education Ecosystem. ​​Mr. Ray has also developed the proprietary UBTECH AI Robotic Education Pedagogy – Acquire Apply Innovate (A2I). The pedagogy elucidates the principles and objectives of our AI Robotic Education across the K12 education.

In 2015, Mr. Ray left the navy to pursue his passion in Education and Training. Till date, Mr. Ray operates two premium MindChamps preschools in Singapore, grooming nearly 300 children yearly. He is the founder and CEO of One World Education Solution in Singapore focusing on Corporate and Adult training. Apart from Mr. Ray’s passion in education, he is also a strong believer that technologies are the key transformation forces of a nation under Industrial 4.0 Revolution.

In 2016, he concurrently assumed the Vice President of Allthing Inc, an Internet of Thing Company in Singapore and China, focusing on Smart Logistic and Smart Energy solutions. His experiences in the education and IOT technology industries have prepared and poised him to be one of the key drivers in UBTECH to develop, build and implement UBTECH End-to-End AI Robotic Education Solution globally..
